Character Creation

This page will help you create your very own unique character which will be used in this game. Depending on the choices you'll make, your game experience will be altered. Choose wisely, but remember that you can always create a new character on this page.


Your character will have a couple of unique hallmarks. These include:

  • Your name
  • Your gender
  • Your class (warrior, mage, thief or cleric)

Who your character will be is up to you. Welcome to JS-RPG!
